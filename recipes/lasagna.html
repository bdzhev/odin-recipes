Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Mozarella
  • Tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Pepper
  • Basil

Method

  1. Boil the sheets in salty water for 2 minutes
  2. Crush and cook the tomatoes with the basil leaves
  3. Layer the sheets on a tray with crushed tomato sauce and cheese
  4. Bake until done
